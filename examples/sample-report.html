情景、分歧与证伪

结论不只看 bull case，也写清楚什么会让它失效。

Bull

Margin stabilizes while energy and software optionality gain evidence.

关键假设: No further severe price war; storage deployments scale.

证伪: Margin keeps falling despite volume.

Base

Auto remains under pressure, energy helps but does not fully re-rate the stock.

关键假设: Mixed demand, gradual cost improvement.

证伪: Either demand snaps back or cash flow deteriorates sharply.

Bear

Pricing pressure and capex overwhelm long-duration optionality.

关键假设: Lower ASP, weaker cash conversion, delayed autonomy monetization.

证伪: Verified high-margin software/energy contribution arrives earlier.
